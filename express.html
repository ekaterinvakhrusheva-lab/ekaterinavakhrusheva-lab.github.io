Overview

I led the redesign of an enterprise communication platform with messaging, calls, and collaboration tools. After 8+ years without significant updates, the product accumulated outdated UI patterns and increasingly complex navigation.

My focus was not just visual refresh, but structural clarity — simplifying core workflows, improving accessibility, and building a scalable design system to support faster delivery across teams.

Challenge

eXpress hadn’t been redesigned in over 8 years. While the product was functionally robust, the experience became harder to navigate over time — patterns diverged across modules, key actions were buried, and everyday flows took too many steps.

The result was increased cognitive load: users spent time searching instead of communicating, and new features were harder to discover and adopt.

Research

To understand what was slowing users down, I combined an audit of key journeys with scaled feedback (survey + interviews), then iterated solutions through prototyping and usability testing.

Solution

Navigation efficiency & Information hierarchy

A simplified navigation and clearer information hierarchy surfaced key features faster, reduced cognitive load, and enabled users to complete tasks more efficiently.

Before
After

Personalized UI & Feature onboarding

We introduced light personalization options to make the interface feel more user-centric. Short onboarding flows helped new users understand features immediately instead of discovering them by accident.

After

Consistency & Scalability

A unified design system aligned components across modules and reduced UI drift. This not only improved visual consistency but also accelerated delivery across teams.

Impact

After rollout, we tracked usability outcomes and sentiment shifts. The biggest win was making daily actions faster and more predictable.
